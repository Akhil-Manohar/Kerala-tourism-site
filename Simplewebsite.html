Top 3 Places to Visit in Kerala

1. Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

  • Architectural Buildings
  • Religious Site

The Padmanabhaswamy Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. Known for its rich history, it's built in Kerala-Dravidian style and is considered the richest Hindu temple. See more

2. Varkala Beach

  • Beaches

Varkala is a quiet hamlet with a serene beach, a 2000-year-old temple, and the peaceful Sivagiri Ashram nearby. See more

3. Alleppey Backwaters

  • Bodies of Water

The Alleppey backwaters are a network of lakes and canals stretching over 900 km, perfect for houseboat cruises through Kerala's lush green countryside. See more
